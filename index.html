About me

My name is Janine Kostka and I'm from a small town in southern Germany. I've always been tech-savy but my interest in programming began with a school project in which we had to program a small self-built remote race car to drive around a course autonomously. After that I've been playing around with some Java writing Android Apps.
Right now, I'm learning to build web apps with JavaScript.
A probably very strange hobby of mine is mechanical keyboards. After stumbling upon the subreddit and reading a lot of forum posts on Geekhack I've began building and modding them myself. It's pretty fun coming up with a new keyboard layout or tinkering around with PCB design.
When I'm not doing nerdy things I'm also an avid concert-goer. Specifically going to smallish club shows. If you've never been to one, try it.
